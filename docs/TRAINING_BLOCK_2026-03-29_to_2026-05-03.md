# Training Block: 2026-03-29 To 2026-05-03

This block is the starting structure for the run-in toward:

- `Silver Rush 50 MTB` on `2026-07-12`
- `Park City Point 2 Point` on `2026-09-05`

The priority for this block is:

- improve long climbing durability
- keep easy days truly easy at altitude
- continue raising uphill cadence without losing climbing specificity
- preserve `2` heavy / low-rep strength sessions per week when practical
- begin practicing race-realistic fueling on longer rides

## Block Rules

- In Park City, easy days should stay below about `150-155 bpm`.
- In Memphis, use the Dr. Testa flat workout templates directly.
- Keep at least `24` hours between heavy lower-body lifting and the main interval session when possible.
- Use liquids plus gels on long rides and start measuring what intake actually works.

## What This Block Is Trying To Fix

- The current weekly climbing load is improving, but it is spread across shorter rides rather than sustained long rides.
- Easy altitude rides have been drifting too hard.
- Climbing cadence is improving, but it is not yet stable in the target range under fatigue.
- The biggest gap to both goal races is long-duration climbing durability, not willingness to suffer on short hills.
